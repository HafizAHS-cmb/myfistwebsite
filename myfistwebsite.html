About Me

Hafiz Abdul Hamid Salifu, born on September 19, 2000, in Ejisu, Ashanti, Ghana, is a distinguished author, researcher, and political analyst specializing in African politics, environmental governance, and social justice. He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Ghana, where he studied Geography and Political Science. Salifu's notable work, "Geo-Environmental Political Adaptation," integrates environmental determinism into political ideologies and governance structures in Africa, offering innovative perspectives on how geographic and climatic realities can influence governance to foster stability and sustainable development.
